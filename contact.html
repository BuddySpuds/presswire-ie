Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly is my PR published?

Your press release goes live in 60 seconds after payment confirmation, complete with SEO-optimized backlinks.

Can I edit my PR after publishing?

Yes, you can edit your PR within 24 hours of publication using your management link. This includes updating links and content.

What SEO benefits do I get?

All links are dofollow, passing full SEO value. You can add custom anchor text, multiple backlinks, keywords, and tag by industry/location for maximum visibility.

How do discount codes work?

Enter your discount code at checkout to instantly reduce your price. Codes can offer 10-100% off and are provided by our admin team or through special promotions.

Can I add images or videos to my PR?

Yes! Add links to high-res images or videos. PRs with media get 48% more engagement and shares.

How does categorization help my PR?

Tag your PR by industry, type, and region. This helps journalists and readers find relevant news, and enables future filtering/subscription features.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and debit cards through Stripe. Secure, instant processing.

Do you verify all companies?

Yes, we verify your email domain and cross-check with the Companies Registration Office (CRO) database to ensure authenticity.

Can I schedule a PR for later?

Currently PRs publish immediately. Embargo/scheduling features are coming soon!
